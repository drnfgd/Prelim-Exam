Flavor: Brazo De Mercedes

The Amigas‘ Brazo de Mercedes is made from a soft meringue with custard filling and dusted with confectioners‘ sugar. This cupcake is not too typical as it comes with rich and flavorful custard filling that will surely be worth it. Brazo de Mercedes is one of the Filipinos‘ all-time favorite sweet treats. This delectable round cupcake measures exactly 3 ounces and 3 inches in height, and will be available in half-dozen box, secured in a cupcake paper box with a plastic carrier bag.

About

Amigas Treats
What is Amigas cupcake?

The Amigas cupcakes were derived from traditional Filipino desserts—Yema and Brazo de Mercedes. The Yema Cupcake is a chiffon-based cupcake with yema frosting while the Amigas Brazo Cupcake is made of meringue with filling dusted with confectioner sugar – available in three (4) flavors: Original Custard, Purple Yam/ Ube, Coffee, and Strawberry.


Mission

ACDG Group Co.‘s mission is to bake nourishing, local treats that are accessible and inclusive for everyone. The company is committed in making the best quality offerings that will make every eating experience more satisfying and comforting while exceeding customer expectations.


Vision

ACDG Group Co.‘s vision is to become an exceptional company in creating and innovating flavorful treats. The company aspires to become one of the most recommended brands in town due to the remarkable and delicious taste of its offerings.


The Company

ACDG Group Co. is a company that manufactures traditional Filipino cupcakes which will be offered in four (4) flavors: 1. Brazo de Mercedes, 2.Brazo de Kape, 3. Brazo de Violeta, ang Brazo de Fresa. These products will bear the brand name ― Amigas.
